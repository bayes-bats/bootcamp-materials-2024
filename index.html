The next four topics are based on the open access intro statistics textbook OpenIntro IMS. OpenIntro IMS has an emphasis on simulation-based inference, and hence you will see terms such as bootstrap in many chapters (e.g., Chapters 11 and 12). For the purpose of preparing for Bayes BATS bootcamp, we will focus on Central Limit Theorem-based inference, also called mathematical modeling by OpenInro IMS, as in Chapter 13. As you go over Chapters 14 and 16, you will encounter bootstrap methods and donâ€™t worry if you donâ€™t have the time to fully digest these portions.
